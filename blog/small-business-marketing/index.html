2. Master Local SEO

Local SEO helps you appear in search results when people look for products or services in your area. Beyond your Google Business Profile, here's how to dominate local search:

Create Location-Specific Content

Write blog posts and pages targeting local keywords: "Best [product] in [City]", "[Your service] in [Neighborhood]", etc.
