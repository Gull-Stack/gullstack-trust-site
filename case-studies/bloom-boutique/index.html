The Challenge

Bloom Boutique is a women's clothing store in a trendy downtown district. Owner Jessica Chen curates a beautiful collection of contemporary fashion, but she was watching customers browse in-store, then buy online from bigger retailers.

"I'd see customers take photos of our pieces, then order the same thing from Nordstrom or ASOS," Jessica explains. "We were basically a showroom for other people's online sales."

Jessica knew she needed to sell online, but the numbers didn't work:

  • E-commerce website quote: $8,000-15,000 upfront
  • Social media management: $1,500-2,500/month
  • Photography for products: $2,000-5,000
  • Paid advertising: $500-2,000/month minimum
  • Total first-year investment: $40,000+

"As a single-location boutique, I couldn't risk that kind of money. What if it didn't work?"
